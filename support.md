# PilotCalc Pro — Support

Thanks for using **PilotCalc Pro**, a fast, offline-first flight calculator for pilots.

## Get help

Questions, bug reports, or feature requests? Email **contactvinayak@gmail.com** and
include:

- What you were trying to do
- Which calculator / screen
- What you expected vs. what happened
- Your device and iOS version (e.g. iPhone 15 Pro, iOS 18.6)

## Frequently asked

**Do I need an account or internet connection?**
No. PilotCalc Pro works fully offline with no account. If you're signed in to
iCloud, your saved data syncs privately across your devices.

**Is my data private?**
Yes. The app collects nothing. See the [Privacy Policy](https://callsignsuper.github.io/pilotcalc-privacy/).

**Are the results official?**
No. All outputs — including regulatory figures such as flight-duty-time limits —
are **advisory** planning aids only, and are not a substitute for official
aeronautical information, approved aircraft documentation, your operator's
Operations Manual, or current charts and NOTAMs. The pilot in command is solely
responsible for all operational decisions.
