# Privacy Policy — PilotCalc Pro

**Effective date:** 29 May 2026

PilotCalc Pro is designed to respect your privacy completely. The short version:
**we do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no
accounts, no ads, and no tracking of any kind.**

## What we collect

**Nothing.** PilotCalc Pro does not collect personal information. The app has no
analytics, no advertising, no third-party tracking SDKs, and no telemetry.

## Data you create in the app

Any data you enter or save — calculator inputs, saved trip briefs, aircraft and
operator profiles, crew-rest plans — is stored:

- **On your device**, protected by iOS file-level encryption; and
- **In your own private iCloud account** (Apple CloudKit), if you are signed in
  to iCloud, so it can sync across your devices.

This data lives in **your** private iCloud database. The developer has **no
access** to it and cannot read, retrieve, or share it. iCloud sync is governed by
[Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). If you are not
signed in to iCloud, the app works fully and keeps everything on-device only.

## Network use

PilotCalc Pro works offline. The only data that ever leaves your device is the
information you choose to sync to **your own** private iCloud account, as
described above. We operate no servers and receive no data from the app.

## Notifications

Crew-rest features may schedule **local** notifications (e.g. a rest-end alarm) on
your device. These are generated and delivered entirely on-device and are not sent
to or through us.

## Third-party data

The app bundles publicly available airport reference data from
[OurAirports](https://ourairports.com) (public domain). No data is sent to
OurAirports or any third party.

## Children's privacy

PilotCalc Pro does not collect data from anyone, including children, and is safe
for all ages.

## Your control over your data

Because nothing is collected by us, there is nothing for us to delete. To remove
data you created: delete it within the app, or delete the app and remove its data
from iCloud via **Settings → [your name] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage**.

## Advisory-use disclaimer

PilotCalc Pro is a planning and educational aid. Its outputs — including all
regulatory figures such as flight-duty-time limits — are advisory and are not a
substitute for official aeronautical information, approved aircraft documentation,
your operator's Operations Manual, or current charts and NOTAMs. The pilot in
command is solely responsible for all operational decisions.
